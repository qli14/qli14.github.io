Maxwell equations

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
\nabla\cdot\vec{E} &=& \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0} \\
\nabla\cdot\vec{B} &=& 0 \\
\nabla\times\vec{E} &=& -\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t} \\
\nabla\times\vec{B} &=& \mu_0\left(\vec{J}+\epsilon_0\frac{\partial \vec{E}}{\partial t} \right)
\end{eqnarray}
$$
